# FitForge - App Descriptions for Play Store

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Track, log, and analyze your strength training progress with precision.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

### Headline
FitForge is the app for serious strength trainers who want to track their progress.

### Main Description
FitForge is dedicated to one thing: helping you systematically log your weight training sessions and understand your progress over time. Unlike running-focused fitness apps, FitForge is built from the ground up for strength training.

### Key Features
• **Live Workout Logging** - Log sets, reps, and weight as you train with an intuitive, gym-optimized interface
• **Custom Routines** - Save and reuse your favorite workout routines (push day, pull day, full body, and more)
• **Freeform Logging** - Log workouts however you want, whenever you want (before, during, or after training)
• **Comprehensive History** - Access your complete workout history with detailed breakdowns of every session
• **Progress Analytics** - Track your progression over days and months with visual statistics
• **Calendar View** - See your consistency at a glance with a calendar overview of your training
• **Profile Customization** - Manage your profile and upload your photo
• **Offline-First** - Works completely offline with sync capabilities
• **No Ads, No Paywalls** - Everything essential is free

### Who Is This For?
Whether you're a beginner starting your strength training journey or an experienced lifter fine-tuning your routine, FitForge adapts to your needs. If you care about tracking your lifts and seeing progress, FitForge is for you.

### Why FitForge?
There's no good, dedicated app for strength training tracking. Most fitness apps are either running-focused or overwhelmingly complex with meal plans and macro tracking. FitForge does one thing, and does it well: help you log and track your strength training.

### Technical Details
- Minimal data collection - your workouts stay private
- Works offline - log even without internet
- Clean, intuitive interface optimized for the gym
- Built with React Native for seamless iOS and Android experience

Start logging your strength training today with FitForge!
